HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to The Puzzle Game! Where you can use your mouse to solve a series of puzzles for an EPIC REWARD! Before you begin keep a few things in mind:

1- Hover your mouse over things before clicking them.

2- Keep an eye out for clues to the mysterious code.

3- Clicking on things can reveal clues or take you to a new place check the name of the tab to see where you are.

And last but not least HAVE FUN!
